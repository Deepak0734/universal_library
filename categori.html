Mathematic

Mathematics is the study of numbers, shapes, patterns, and logical reasoning.
It forms the foundation for many fields, including science,engineering,and technology.
Key branches include arithmetic (basic calculations),
algebra (solving equations), geometry (study of shapes), calculus (change and motion), and statistics (data analysis).
Mathematics is not just about numbers; it's a language that helps us understand and describe the world around us, solving problems from simple everyday tasks to complex scientific discoveries.
